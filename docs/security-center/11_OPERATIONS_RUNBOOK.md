# Operations Runbook

## Daily

Check the dashboard. Review critical alerts. Check Defender CVE tickets. Check failed or missing backups. Check parser warnings and failed parser runs. Confirm new Evidence Containers and remediation tickets look accurate.

## Weekly

Review suppressions. Review noisy rules. Review backup trends. Review unresolved tickets. Confirm recurring Defender findings are aggregating rather than creating duplicate tickets. Validate WatchGuard and Backup/NAS inputs are still arriving on schedule.

## Monthly

Review thresholds. Review evidence retention. Review KPI trends. Review audit logs. Prepare audit or management reports. Confirm source owners, parser owners, notification channels, and ticketing responsibilities are still current.

## Escalation

Escalate critical Defender CVEs with exposed devices, repeated failed backups for critical assets, missing backups beyond agreed recovery windows, WatchGuard Zero-Day APT or ThreatSync critical incidents, and parser failures that hide critical sources.
